About Me

I am a Computer Science graduate student at Johns Hopkins University with a passion for pushing the boundaries of Artificial Intelligence. My research interests span across Applied Machine Learning, Large Language Models, Computer Vision, and Quantum Machine Learning, where I focus on developing efficient and scalable AI solutions.

With a track record of publications in prestigious journals and successful project implementations, I combine theoretical knowledge with practical applications. My work includes developing novel frameworks for clinical language models, implementing quantum-classical knowledge transfer, and creating efficient AI systems for real-world applications.
