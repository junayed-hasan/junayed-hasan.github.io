## Accessible Healthcare Platform

Disability Care is a web application designed to bridge the gap between people with disabilities and healthcare services. The platform connects users with hospitals, doctors, pharmacies, and diagnostic centers through an intuitive, accessible, guided interface.

### Key Features

- **Accessible Design**: WCAG-compliant interface for users with various disabilities
- **Healthcare Directory**: Comprehensive database of healthcare providers
- **Guided Navigation**: Step-by-step assistance for booking appointments and services
- **Self-Reliant Experience**: Empowers users to manage their healthcare independently
- **Multi-Service Integration**: Connects hospitals, doctors, pharmacies, and diagnostic centers

### Technical Stack

**Frontend**: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (accessible components)
**Backend**: Django, Python
**Database**: MySQL
**Accessibility**: Screen reader compatible, keyboard navigation, high contrast modes

### Impact

This platform addresses the critical need for accessible healthcare technology, enabling people with disabilities to independently access and manage their healthcare needs without barriers.
