### Review Philosophy

I approach peer review as a constructive dialogue, focusing on:
- <i class="fas fa-check"></i> **Rigor**: Thorough evaluation of methodology and claims
- <i class="fas fa-check"></i> **Constructiveness**: Actionable feedback for improvement
- <i class="fas fa-check"></i> **Timeliness**: Respecting deadlines and author timelines
- <i class="fas fa-check"></i> **Fairness**: Objective assessment free from bias
