Review Philosophy

I approach peer review as a constructive dialogue, focusing on:

  • Rigor: Thorough evaluation of methodology and claims
  • Constructiveness: Actionable feedback for improvement
  • Timeliness: Respecting deadlines and author timelines
  • Fairness: Objective assessment free from bias
